Tortellini Soup

Description

There's nothing quite like a warm bowl of tortellini soup to feel cozy and satisfied. This dish is incredibly easy to make, coming together quickly in one pot. It combines a rich broth with soft, cheese-filled tortellini and fresh vegetables like spinach or carrots for a complete meal.

It's the ideal dinner for a cold winter evening, warming you up from the inside out. It's also the ultimate comfort food for when you're feeling a bit under the weather or simply need a quick, hearty lunch that feels like a hug in a bowl.

Ingredients

  • 2 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
  • 1 (16 ounce) package cheese tortellini
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can Italian-style diced tomatoes
  • 1 (15.5 ounce) can cannellini beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1/2 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinaigrette
  • 1 teaspoon basil
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese, or to taste

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Bring chicken broth to a boil in a large pot; cook tortellini in boiling broth until tender, about 6 minutes.
  3. Stir tomatoes, beans, zucchini, red wine vinaigrette, basil, and black pepper into the broth; cook until the zucchini is tender, about 6 minutes more.
  4. Top with Parmesan cheese to serve.
