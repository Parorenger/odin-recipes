Hummus Pizza

Description

Imagine a pizza that's creamy, crunchy, and bursting with fresh, Mediterranean flavor. That's exactly what you get with a hummus pizza. This creative meal is incredibly easy to assemble and is a true delight for the senses.

Instead of sauce, a savory hummus base is spread over a warm naan or flatbread. Then, it's piled high with vibrant toppings like cherry tomatoes, crisp red onion, salty feta cheese, and fresh parsley. It's the perfect meal for a summer evening, a healthy lunch that won't weigh you down, or a unique and colorful appetizer at your next party that is sure to impress your guests.

Ingredients

  • 1 (10 ounce) can refrigerated pizza crust dough
  • 1 cup hummus spread
  • 1 Â½ cups sliced bell peppers, any color
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475 degrees C (220 degrees C).
  2. Roll out pizza crust and place on a pizza pan or baking sheet. Spread a thin layer of hummus over the crust. Arrange sliced peppers and broccoli over the hummus, and top with shredded cheese.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until the crust is golden brown and cheese is melted in the center, 10 to 15 minutes. Slice and serve.
